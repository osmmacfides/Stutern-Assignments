THE BOX MODELING
Padding:
padding is the space between the content and the Border of a content box. It is used to create space between the content and the border of a content box.
Border:
Border is the line that surrounds the content and padding of a content box. It is used to create a line
Margin:
Margin is the space between the border and the other content boxes. It is used to create space between the border and the other content boxes.
